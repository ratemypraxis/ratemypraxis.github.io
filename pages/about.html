About

Hi, I'm a communications ~generalist~ and researcher living and working in the Eastern Coast of the United States. I'm interested in creative, accessible responses to issues at the intersection of technology and human rights. Lately I have focused on exploring inequity and education surrounding the expansion of surveillance infrastructrue as well as issues of quality and access in connectivity. You can learn more about my recently shared graduate thesis discernible.tech from this presentation video or on its documentation site.
